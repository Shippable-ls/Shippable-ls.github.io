ICO Overview 

The ICO will begin on December 20th 2017 - 12pm GMT.
The pre-sale will begin on November 20th 2017 - 12pm GMT.

Token's will be released 1 month after the ICO closing date. All participants will have the opportunity to use R_Block tokens to pay for activities in the platform. R_Block will be listed on multiple exchanges to ensure the liquidity of the token.

The R_Block token is an ERC20 token. We will be accepting Ether during the token sale. Dividends will be paid to those who hold the coin long term or re-circulate. Protecting the R_Block community is of upmost importance to us.
